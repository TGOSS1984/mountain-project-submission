About

Embark on a breathtaking journey through the UK’s most iconic winter landscapes with Tom Goss' Guided Winter Hikes. Whether you’re an experienced hiker or a beginner looking for your next adventure, Tom’s expert guidance will ensure a memorable experience amidst the beauty of winter’s embrace. From tranquil walks through snow-covered valleys to exhilarating winter scrambles, there’s something for every skill level, ensuring you challenge yourself in an environment that’s as breathtaking as it is rewarding.

Tom, a seasoned hiker and passionate outdoor enthusiast, brings years of knowledge and expertise to every guided trek. He has hand-picked some of the UK’s most famous hiking routes, offering you the opportunity to explore well-known trails like the Lake District, the Snowdonia National Park, and Scotland’s rugged Highlands—all while witnessing the serene charm that winter brings to these majestic locations. Whether you’re interested in learning essential winter mountain skills for safe and confident hiking in icy conditions or simply wish to experience the quiet beauty of winter, Tom tailors each hike to meet your needs.

On these guided hikes, you’ll not only experience stunning views and untouched winter landscapes, but you’ll also enjoy the camaraderie of fellow hikers as you navigate some of the most challenging and rewarding terrain. Tom offers various hiking experiences, from basic winter hiking that introduces you to the fundamentals of winter trekking, to more advanced graded scrambles for those seeking a more physically demanding challenge. Learn essential techniques for safe navigation in snow and ice, and gain confidence in your ability to tackle more technical routes as you work with Tom's expert instruction.

Whether you're scaling Snowdon in the crisp winter air, learning new mountain skills in the heart of Snowdonia, or tackling the snow-covered peaks of the Lake District with a graded scramble, Tom's guidance ensures safety, enjoyment, and unforgettable memories every step of the way. His focus on building both skill and confidence means you'll leave each hike feeling empowered and ready for your next winter adventure.

Join us for a winter adventure that will stay with you long after you’ve returned home. Whether you're new to winter hiking or looking to develop your skills in more technical terrain, our guided experiences in the UK’s finest trails offer the perfect mix of challenge and reward. Discover new heights, learn crucial winter hiking techniques, and make your winter hiking experience one to remember.

Regions To Explore

Mountains of Wales

The Brecon Beacons & Snowdonia National park contain some of the Uks classic routes, including tryfan & for those with a head for heights, Crib Goch, the most exicting route up to Snowdon!

Mountains of Scotland

The Scottish Mountains can be some of the most wild in the UK. From the Caingorms to Glencoe & the Isle of Skye, there is something for everybody

Mountains of the Lake District

Explore the Wainwirght's, with classic routes & various grades of scrambling. From the classic ridges of Helvellyn to the Scafell range including Englands highest peak

Mountains of the Peak District

Explore Kinder Scout, a vast platauex with many classic routes to the summit or the famous ridgeline of Mam Tor!
